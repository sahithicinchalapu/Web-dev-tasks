  Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is called the "City of Destiny" for a few key reasons, rooted in both historical prophecy and the city's actual growth and development. The name is attributed to a dream or vision experienced by ruler Krishnadevaraya of the Vijayanagara Empire, foretelling a great city's establishment there. Furthermore, the city's namesake, Visakha, the Hindu God of courage, is often associated with the belief that destiny favors the brave, adding to the symbolic "destiny" connection.
